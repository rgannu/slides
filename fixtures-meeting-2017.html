Player movement restrictions

  • During the BCF season a player is not allowed to play anywhere else
  • If he wants he can get an NOC from BCF League Chairman and go
  • Player once leaves BCF during the season CANNOT come back for the same season
  • When a player comes from another Organization to BCF he must get an NOC from their Club/Federation
    • The other organization will be informed by Email
